Hello everyone!

My name is Ari, the author and developer of HardMon and HardMonLib. I'm a software developer from Lombok. I began programming actively in 2011 and have since developed various utility applications such as uDiscMounter, DNSwift, uBattery, and many others.

Let me share a bit about the story behind HardMon and HardMonLib.

I initially started working on hardware monitoring software back in late 2014 with a project called ACPi Info, which later became the foundation for what is now HardMon. However, due to work commitments and active involvement in outdoor activities with my organization, I had to put the project on hold.

From early 2020 to 2024, I resumed the project under a new name — HardMon (Hardware Monitor) — adding new features and support for a wide range of Intel and AMD motherboard sensors. Alongside this, I also developed HardMonLib, a companion library designed for use by other developers. Since then, I’ve continued to enhance and refine both projects.
