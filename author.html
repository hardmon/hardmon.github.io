HardMon

Hello everyone! I am Ari, author and developer of HardMon and HardEngine. I am a software developer from a small island in the Nusa Tenggara archipelago of Indonesia. I started actively programming in 2011 and have developed many utility software programs such as uDiscMounter, DNSwift, uBattery, and many more.

Let me tell you a little bit about why I developed HardMon and HardEngine.

I actually started developing hardware monitoring software since the end of 2014 which I named ACPi Info (the forerunner of HardMon) and in that year I stopped continuing the project due to being busy with work in the real world and participating in many nature activities with my organization, until in early 2020 to 2024 I started thinking about continuing the project with a different name to HardMon (Hardware Monitor) by completing various features and supporting almost all sensors on Intel and AMD Motherboards and also creating a library called HardEngine which can be used by developers, until now I continue to improve and perfect the HardMon project.
